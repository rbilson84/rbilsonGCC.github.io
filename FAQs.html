Frequently Asked Questions

They're not frequently asked, but doesnt hurt to have some

Do you offer block booking discounts?

Sadly not. Unfortunately, the more visits required doesn't mean our costs reduce.

Do you charge extra for multi-cat households?

No, the quoted price is per household.

Do you charge extra for administering medications?

No, this is also inclusive of our visit.

Can you water plants and take mail in while i am away?

Yes, not a problem.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash and bank transfers. Contactless payments should be available shortly.

What is your cancellation policy?

At the moment we do not currently have one. We understand that plans can change last minute. We may review this over the christmas period.

Do you look after other small pets?

Yes, more than happy to.

If you become unwell, will my pet still be cared for?

Yes, my partner David is insured through the business insurance and is available to step-in and is available to help during busy periods

What if my pet passes away while i am away?

In the sad event that this may happen, we will keep in contact with you and help take them to their vets
