Rachel's Rascals Pet Sitting

Cat sitters in Glasgow East End

Looking after your pets with love, while you are away


Since 2021, we have been serving the pets and families of our local area. We love caring and connecting with animals and creating everlasting relationships.

We provide care for your pets as if they were our own, and ensure that they receive the highest quality care while you are away. Each visit includes an update via Whatsapp with pictures and videos.

Each visit includes a treat (or few) as i feel this helps create a positive experience when we visit. If you would prefer us not to use treats, please let us know.
